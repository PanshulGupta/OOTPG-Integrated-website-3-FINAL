Although Kids Wish network had fulfilled over 800 wishes before 2013, since 2014, former employees had confessed that only 60-70 wishes were fulfilled ever since, per annum on sick children, and over 10% of this figure describes that wishes were granted by providing objects, such as toys, clothes, etc. instead of an access to healthcare, and an exemplary medical aid. Instead of this, some of the wishes required the children to provide the money, by employees being forced to persuade children that they will be cured if they provide money, the money that was provided by the children, were used in majorly paying the individuals that had collected/convinced donors, to donate in the charity, while over 30% of the money given was then used to run the company, and improve services, but not the children's health and wishes.

 

Alongside using children, one of the significant reasons that had made Kids Wish Network infamous, could be seen when only $240,000 dollars, of the $18.6 million dollars, raised in 2012, were used, on fulfilling the ‘Kids’ Wishes’, hence, in 2013, it was calculated that less than 12.24% of every dollar is spent primarily on the reason why the organization was created in the first place. The rest of the money, that was not utilized for the organizations primary reason, was invested in the services of the companies co-founded, and founded by the founder of the charity, Mark Breiner. In 2011, approximately $13 million of the $19.4 million dollars raised, were invested in other forms that will be mentioned.
